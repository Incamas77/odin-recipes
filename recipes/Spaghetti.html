Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti! a timeless dish that creates full stomachs and warm hearts in every families household

A nice and easy meal tio prep with a small selection of ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground meat (any ground meat will work)
  • Tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Onion, garlic, and spices

Steps

  1. Start with the sauce: Brown your meat in a bit of oil, and then stir in onions until they soften. Then add lots of flavor with garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes. To finish the sauce, add a bit of water (or broth) and crushed tomatoes. Our easy sauce simmers for about 25 minutes, so about 15 minutes before it is ready, you can cook your spaghetti.
  2. Cook the noodles: Pasta water should taste salty. I add a lot of salt to our pasta water (it makes the pasta taste delicious). I add about one tablespoon of salt for every four quarts (16 cups) of water, which is perfect for cooking one pound of pasta.If you still have it, check your package instructions.   3. Toss the sauce: When your spaghetti is ready, toss it with your delicious homemade meat sauce and serve with some parmesan cheese sprinkled on top. Yum!
